FAQ'S

What is LIVEWIRE?

LIVEWIRE is a division of CADD Centre is an approved Training Partner of National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) in India. LIVEWIRE offers training in emerging technologies like Data Science, Machine Learning, IoT, Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Full Stack Developer, Cybersecurity, and many more.

Where is LIVEWIRE located?

LIVEWIRE is present in more than 100 locations in India and over 7 countries.

What kind of training is offered at LIVEWIRE?

LIVEWIRE offers training in a variety of areas, including RPA, Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, MEAN Stack Developer, Ethical Hacking, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, IoT, Data Science, PLC & SCADA, Automotive Embedded system, Network Engineering, Cybersecurity, OrCAD Capture and Editor, Linux, C++, Python, and more.

Who prepares the curricula for LIVEWIRE courses?

The training is offered by certified instructors who are certified by the Internal Training Academy (ITA).

Are LIVEWIRE courses accredited?

Yes, LIVEWIRE is affiliated with several accrediting bodies such as NSDC, STED Council, ISO (9001:2015 & 29990:2010), NIELIT, AXELOS, Oracle, CompTIA, Blockchain Council, etc.

What are the facilities offered at LIVEWIRE training centers?

LIVEWIRE training centers boast of high-end hardware and sophisticated technology labs that are modeled on real-world R&D units of industries.

Is LIVEWIRE associated with any colleges or corporates?

Yes, LIVEWIRE works closely with more than 1000 colleges in India as a technology partner in equipping students beyond their academics and also with a few corporate clients for their training requirements.

What is the duration of courses offered by LIVEWIRE?

The duration of courses offered by LIVEWIRE ranges from 20 hours to 160 hours, depending on the course.

Does LIVEWIRE offer placement assistance?

Yes, LIVEWIRE offers placements and internships and has successfully placed close to 3000 students.
